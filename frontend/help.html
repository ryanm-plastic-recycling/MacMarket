MacMarket Help

Dashboard

The main dashboard shows a scrolling ticker, quick price tiles and news feeds. Use the Pause button to stop the ticker. Tiles show the first four symbols from your saved list.

Login

Enter your username and password to sign in. If OTP is enabled on your account, provide the six digit code. CAPTCHA can be disabled in local testing.

Account

Update your username, email and password. Enable two-factor authentication by checking Enable OTP. A link to manage tickers and, if you are an administrator, to the admin panel is provided.

Tickers

Manage the list of symbols tracked across the site. Add or remove tickers and press Save to store them. These symbols populate the dashboard ticker and drive recommendation data.

Signals

Retrieve trading insights. Enter a symbol and click Get Signal to see news sentiment and a simple technical signal. Paste macroeconomic commentary into the text box and click Analyze for an LLM-generated outlook. Use Get Recommendations to fetch trade suggestions based on your saved tickers.

Journal

Record your trades and review current positions. Existing entries appear in the table. Fill out the form to add a new journal entry.
